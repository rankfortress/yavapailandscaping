FireWise Landscaping is a method of integrating the best practices and standards into the landscape of your home or commercial landscape. The main goal of FireWise Landscaping is to remove anything that could constitute fire hazardous material or vegetation and by creating a defensible space around your home, other structures and mature trees. There are a lot of things to consider when it comes to FireWise and Safety and most people see it as daunting. With Yavapai Landscaping, it becomes easy! Creating a defensible space isn’t by razing every living thing around your home, different terrains and spaces require different strategies.
